Conclusion

The exercises suggested in this booklet need to be modified according to needs. These exercises have been suggested to clarify the concept of motor fitness training. There are numerous exercises the coach can create to improve the motor fitness of the players. While designing exercises coaches must consider recovery, quality of effort, individual ability and the effect of the exercise on the player. Finally, for all court work: shadow. Multifeeding, specific pattern drills and match play is agility.
